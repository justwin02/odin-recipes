Lasagna Recipe

Description:

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering wide, flat pasta sheets with rich fillings such as meat sauce, cheese, and sometimes vegetables, then baking it until hot and bubbling.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Meat sauce
  • Cheese Mixture
  • Mozzarella
  • Topping

Steps:

  1. Preheat Oven
  2. Cook the Meat
  3. Boil the Noodles
  4. Mix the Ricotta filling
  5. Assemble the Lasagna
  6. Bake
