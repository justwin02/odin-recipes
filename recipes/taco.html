Taco Recipe

Description:

A taco is a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a folded or rolled tortilla—usually made from corn or flour—that is filled with a variety of ingredients. Common fillings include grilled or stewed meats (like beef, chicken, pork, or fish), beans, cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, salsa, onions, and guacamole. Tacos are often garnished with cilantro, lime juice, and hot sauces, offering a flavorful and customizable eating experience.

Tacos can be soft (with a pliable tortilla) or crispy (typically deep-fried or baked), and they’re popular in both street food and home-cooked settings. With countless regional and modern variations—like tacos al pastor, fish tacos, or breakfast tacos—they’re loved for their versatility, portability, and bold flavors.
