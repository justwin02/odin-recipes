Burger Recipe

Description:

A burger (short for hamburger) is a popular sandwich that typically consists of a ground meat patty—most commonly beef—served inside a sliced bun. It’s often grilled or pan-fried and can be customized with a wide variety of toppings and condiments.

Ingredients:

  • Bun
  • Patty
  • Lettuce/Tomato/Onion/Pickles/Cheese
  • Ketcup/Mustard/Mayo

Steps:

  1. Form the Patties
  2. Season the Patties
  3. Cook Patties
  4. Toast Buns
  5. ASsemble the Burger
